FaSTCC: Fast Sparse Tensor Contractions on CPUs

Add your representative image here

Authors: Saurabh Raje, Hunter McCoy, Prashant Pandey, Atanas Rountev, P. Sadayappan

Status: Under Review

Abstract

Developed a new library for performing sparse tensor contractions. We implement a tiled outer-outer scheme with automatic prediction of the optimal tile sizes. This library, fastCC, achieves speedups of up to 11 times over the state of the art on real-world benchmarks.
