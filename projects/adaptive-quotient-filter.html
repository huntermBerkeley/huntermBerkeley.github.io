Authors: Richard Wen, David Tench, Hunter McCoy, Guido Tagliavini, Michael A. Bender, Alex Conway, Martin Farach-Colton, Rob Johnson, Prashant Pandey

Venue: SIGMOD 2025

Abstract

Designed the Adaptive Quotient Filter (AQF), the first filter with strong adaptivity. The AQF is 10 times faster than other adaptive filter designs for insertion and the same throughput for queries while also being dynamic, mergeable, resizable, and giving stronger error guarantees.
